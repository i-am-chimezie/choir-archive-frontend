About the Archive

Preserving Nigeria's sacred choral tradition for future generations of worshippers, choirmasters, and music lovers.

Our Mission

The Nigerian Catholic Choir Archive is a digital repository dedicated to collecting, preserving, and sharing the rich tradition of Catholic choral music from Nigeria. From the iconic compositions of Sir Jude Nnam to traditional Igbo hymns, we ensure these sacred works remain accessible to choirs worldwide.

Many Nigerian Catholic choirs struggle to find sheet music for popular songs. This archive solves that problem by providing a centralized, searchable database of choral works.

What We Archive

  • Entrance hymns and processional songs
  • Mass parts (Kyrie, Gloria, Sanctus, Agnus Dei)
  • Offertory and Communion songs
  • Responsorial Psalms and Gospel Acclamations
  • Marian hymns and devotional songs
  • Works in Igbo, English, Latin, and other Nigerian languages
